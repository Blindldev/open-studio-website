Frequently Asked Questions

What are your studio hours?

Our studio is open 24/7 for independent study members. For class participants, hours vary by session. Please check our schedule for specific class times.

Do I need experience to join?

For our independent study program, yes - you need to have completed at least one class at our studio. For beginner classes, no experience is necessary!

What's included in the membership fee?

The $190 monthly fee includes 25 lbs of clay, 24/7 studio access, storage space, and use of all equipment and glazes. Firing services are also included.

How do I join the waitlist?

You can join our waitlist by filling out the form on our website. We'll contact you when a spot becomes available.

Can I bring guests?

Yes, members can bring one guest per visit. Guests are welcome to observe but cannot use the equipment unless they purchase a day pass.

What happens to my work if I can't pick it up?

Work left unclaimed for 30 days will be discarded. We make reasonable efforts to notify members before disposal.

Can I use my own clay?

Only studio-provided clay is permitted for use in the studio. This policy helps protect our equipment and ensure consistent firing results.

How do I cancel my membership?

To cancel, send an email to info@openstudio.com by the 15th of the month prior to your intended cancellation date.
