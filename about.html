Independent Study Program

For dedicated artists ready to take their practice to the next level

Program Overview

Our Independent Study program offers 24/7 studio access and monthly clay with firings and glazes for artists to develop their practice. Members must have completed at least one class at our studio to participate.

Monthly Membership: $190

Automatically billed on the 1st of each month

What's Included:

  • 25 lbs of premium clay per month
  • 24/7 access to our fully-equipped facility
  • Secure 4ft x 2ft storage locker
  • Access to all studio equipment and tools
  • Flexible cancellation policy

Studio Equipment

  • Professional Shimpo VL Whisper Pottery Wheels
  • Premium Laguna Clay bodies
  • Alpine Gas Reduction Kiln
  • Electric Kilns (Skutt/Cone Art)
  • In-house Glazes
  • Natural Light-filled Workspace
  • Stainless Steel Wash Sinks
  • Large Work and Wedging Tables
  • Slab Roller
  • Clay Extruder
  • Trimming and Carving Tools
  • Banding Wheels
