About Open Studio

Chicago's premier 24-hour pottery studio for independent artists

Our Story

In 2024, I (Mike), was hosting popup pottery social events across the city while creating my own work in my garage. That September, I found a commercial lease with plans to simply keep my kiln for these social gatherings. But things took an exciting turn in October 2024 when I hosted my first social event in that very space.

While this space has flourished and I've had the privilege of teaching over 1000 new students, I found myself missing the intimate atmosphere of my "Garage Studio" - that special place where I could lose myself in the rhythm of pottery-making, accompanied by my favorite music for hours on end.

This longing and feedback from students inspired the creation of Throw247. I wanted to offer a dedicated group of passionate creatives their own mini community - a 24-hour space where they could throw pottery whenever inspiration struck. The vision was clear: a cozy, welcoming environment filled with fun plants, personal tools, and thoughtful touches like a record player to set the perfect vibe.

To maintain the intimate atmosphere, the space will begin with just 15 paying members, plus a few select friends and staff. I'm incredibly excited to see how this space evolves and grows! I look forward to taking feedback too and adapting the space to the needs of the community.

Our Space

Located in the heart of Ravenswood, our studio features:

  • 10 pottery wheels (one standing wheel to be added soon)
  • Dedicated handbuilding area
  • Dedicated Electric kiln for low-firing (plans to add larger one soon)
  • Personal storage lockers
  • 24/7 access for members
