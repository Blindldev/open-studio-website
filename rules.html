Studio Rules & Guidelines

Membership Terms

  • Full membership payment is due on the 1st of every month
  • Memberships unpaid by the 5th will expire
  • Cancellation requires email notification by the 15th of the prior month
  • Personal items must be removed by month's end

Studio Clay Usage

  • Only studio-provided clay is permitted
  • 25 lbs of clay included monthly
  • Maximum 50 lbs firing limit per month
  • Outside clay requires written approval

Equipment Usage

  • Clean all equipment after use
  • Report any issues immediately
  • Follow posted safety guidelines
  • Respect shared workspace

Storage Space

  • 4ft x 2ft storage locker per member
  • Keep storage space clean and organized
  • Remove items upon membership cancellation
  • Use communal storage responsibly

Guest Policy

  • Maximum 1 guest per visit
  • Guests must be supervised
  • Day passes available for full studio use
  • Limit of 4 guest passes per month

Cleanliness

  • Clean up after each session
